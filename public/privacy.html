Overview

Investment Co-Pilot is an educational investment analysis app. This starter privacy policy should be reviewed by a qualified professional before public launch.

Information we may collect

We may collect app usage data, watchlist inputs, risk profile selections, device diagnostics, and account details if sign-in is added later.

How information may be used

Information may be used to provide educational analysis, improve product reliability, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Financial data

Do not enter brokerage passwords, bank passwords, private keys, seed phrases, or confidential trading credentials into the app.

AI and market-data providers

If AI or market-data providers are connected, submitted asset data may be processed by those providers to generate analysis. The production version must list each provider and link to their policies.
